The Science of Psychedelic Sound

Spatial Audio & Consciousness

This installation explores how spatial audio processing can alter perception and consciousness. By manipulating phase relationships, frequency content, and temporal delays, we create immersive soundscapes that mirror the perceptual shifts associated with psychedelic experiences.

Hendrix-Inspired Techniques

Drawing from Jimi Hendrix's pioneering use of feedback, distortion, and studio effects, this digital installation recreates and extends those revolutionary techniques through algorithmic composition and real-time audio processing.

Interactive Generative Music

Each interaction triggers algorithmic composition processes that generate unique musical phrases, ensuring no two experiences are identical while maintaining coherent musical structures.
